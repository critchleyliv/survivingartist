Use Your Student Discount

Before shopping at just any location for materials make sure the place you’re shopping at (restraunts, salons, non-art stores, ect) accepts your ID for a discount. We know money is tight, so here is a list of go to locations for using your student ID or free rewards programs.

Jerry's Artarama has a student discount as well as a FREE Vip Discount Card which includes 10% off everything, a special birthday offer, and more. Michael's Rewards programs offers member-only discount codes and other deals available on their rewards app. At Blick's Art Materials, Preferred Customers (students & professionals) can recieve 10% off all products. Join Staples Rewards to get 5% off purchases in store and online as well as free shipping on orders over $14.99.
